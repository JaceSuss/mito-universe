About Us

We are the students of Dela Salle-College of Saint benilde opening another avenue to make Philippine's culture be more known to many. With the urge to bring these characters into limelight, we have reinterpreted these chracters in modern yet rennaissance way of digital sculpture. Some of these artworks are modified from real life existing statues and applied to our setting. If you wish to know more about us... now's the chance..
