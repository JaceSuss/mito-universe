Just like any place, Philippines has a lot to offer from mythical creatures, urban legend and other stories that came to be passed from tongue to tongue. By just hearing stories about a magical golden realm of gold in Samar known as Biringan City chills will definitely crawl into your skin. There are also stories of floating fireball spirits known as Santelmo that can be found in the calm seas or forests in some of the Philippine Islands and these will literally chase the person who sees it.

There are lots of stories and creatures passed through the cultural transference in the Philippines. Take your first step in knowing some of them through creative
3d visualizations.

CREATURES

Aswang

Creatures in the Philippines are more likely related to the Filipino's belief for Aswang or umbrella term for various shape-shifting evil creatures in Filipino folklore, such as vampires, ghouls, witches, viscera suckers and more.

Manananggal Sigbin Tiyanak

DEITIES

Panginoon

Deities in the Philippines are diverse through different cultures from different tribes and places. Just like other popular mythical gods, they also have gods for specific events, phenomenon, or simple things. Some are usually guardians, goddesses, and nymphs who take care of the Philippines rich resources.

Bathala Libulan Makiling

ENGKANTOS

Maligno

Engkantos locally known as “Maligno” are some spirits that also believed to guard some places and natural resources in the Philippines. There are a lot of them circulating around the stories. Some are told through experiencing while it was believed to be passed on the scare children in some bedtime stories.

Bungisngis Tikbalang

Mito Universe was created by Multimedia artists from Dela Salle-College of Saint Benilde artistically curate a story driven visualization through 3d sculptures that was made by the students. These is to promote the rich culture of the Philippine Mythology as a part of vast universe full of magical untold stories left untouched by other generations. Mito Universe will provide a visual experience that will pinch the curiosity of the most imaginative and creative people so as those who just wanted to know the Philippine culture through its stories. We will be your guide through this journey, in a place from Mito-U!
